Academic Programs

Our Curriculum

Hosanna Secondary School offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to meet national education standards while incorporating innovative teaching methodologies. Our program prepares students for both national examinations and global competitiveness.

Science Department

Our science program offers rigorous courses in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics, supported by fully equipped laboratories and experienced faculty.

  • Advanced Physics Lab
  • Chemistry Research Projects
  • Biology Field Studies
  • Mathematics Olympiad Team

Humanities Department

The humanities program fosters critical thinking and cultural awareness through courses in History, Geography, Languages, and Social Sciences.

  • Debate and Model UN
  • Creative Writing Workshops
  • Cultural Exchange Programs
  • Language Immersion

Technical Department

Our technical department prepares students for practical careers with courses in Computer Science, Business Studies, and Applied Sciences.

  • Computer Programming
  • Entrepreneurship Training
  • Technical Drawing
  • Home Science
