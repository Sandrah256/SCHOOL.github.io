Admissions Process

Thank you for considering Hosanna Secondary School for your child's education. Below you'll find all the information you need about our admissions process.

How to Apply

1

Inquiry

Submit an inquiry form either online or in person to receive detailed information about our programs and admission requirements.

2

School Tour

Schedule a visit to our campus to tour our facilities and meet with our admissions team.

3

Application

Complete the application form and submit all required documents along with the application fee.

4

Assessment

Students may be required to take an entrance assessment depending on the grade level.

5

Admission Decision

You will receive an admission decision within two weeks of completing all requirements.

6

Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete the enrollment process by submitting all required forms and fees.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (copy)
  • Previous school reports (last two years)
  • Recommendation letter from previous school
  • Medical report
  • 4 passport-sized photographs
  • Application fee receipt
